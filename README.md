### 🔗 [DevConnect Pro](https://devconnect-pro-v2.vercel.app/)
**The career platform that actually gets what you're worth.**

</div>

> Most job platforms treat your resume like a PDF and your skills like a checkbox. DevConnect Pro doesn't.

A full-stack AI-powered career platform built for developers — combining resume analysis, adaptive mock interviews, and portfolio building in one place.

**What it does:**

- 🧠 **AI Resume Analyzer** — Real, specific feedback. Not "use action verbs."
- ✍️ **Resume Generator** — Polished, ATS-friendly output from your raw input
- 🎤 **Interview Simulator** — Questions based on *your* actual resume, not a generic bank
- 🔁 **Adaptive AI** — Difficulty adjusts in real-time based on your answers
- 🏢 **Company-wise Tracks** — ML Engineer, Web Developer, more coming
- 🗂️ **Portfolio Builder** — Showcase your projects. No design skills needed.
